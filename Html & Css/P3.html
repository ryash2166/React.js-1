THE SPITI VALLEY

Experience Mesmerising Milky Way.

Spiti Valley is a snow desert with huge diversity and beauty in the Himalayas which lies between India and Tibet. With rough terrains, decent roads, waterfalls, rivers, monasteries, hamlets, and a non-polar glacier, Spiti Valley is an offbeat destination for adventure, photography, and meditation.
